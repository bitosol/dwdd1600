Why do They Migrate?

Here are 3 main Reasons:

  1. Seasonal Changes
  2. Habitat Conditions
  3. Food Supply

Where and Why

Where do monarchs spend their time in the Spring and Summer?
Inland of Mexico and the United States to lay their eggs and find milkweed.
Where do monarchs spend their time in the Fall and Winter?
From Cali to Arizona and mostly Mexico, to escape cold weather and enjoy nectar.
